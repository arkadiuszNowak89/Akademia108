About us

The Building Company sources, distributes, wholesales & retails general & specialised building materials. B-ONE specialise in the hiring of accommodation, sanitation and storage facilities that cater for most of the construction and civil engineering sites in Gauteng and surrounding areas. The stores also offer a delivery service from store to door. You can also enquire about; DIY kitchen cupboards – Timbercity offers a comprehensive range of pre-assembled cupboards that are simple and easy to install. Custom designed cupboards, carpentry and cabinetry tools – Timbercity stock all the tools needed to complete any cabinetry or carpentry project. From power tools to levels and drill bits, they have it covered. Wood and board, as wood suppliers, they offer a variety of basic and exotic timber, from boards to veneers. In combination with their cutting service, buying from them could save you time and effort. Enquire in store about their extensive range of board and timber.
